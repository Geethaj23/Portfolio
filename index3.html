List of Awards Received by Dr. APJ Abdul Kalam

From being born in a very poor family to becoming India’s 11th President, Dr APJ Abdul Kalam has shown us that nothing is impossible. And, the list of Awards that Dr Kalam has received will inspire you even more.
Dr Kalam has given an unforgettable contribution to the DRDO (Defence Research and Development Organisation) and ISRO (Indian Space Research Organisation).
Not only this, but he was also deeply involved in the development of Ballistic Missile and Launch Vehicle technology.
In this blog post, I will be listing out all the awards that Dr APJ Abdul Kalam grabbed over years for his great works.





NSS Chairman Mark Hopkins presents Dr Kalam with the NSS Von Braun award for leading India into space and for being a global leader in space development.
